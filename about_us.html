Our Mission

To be a social enterprise that strives to live the values, principles, and practices of good governance and human rights through dedicated community programmes.

Our Programmes

We focus on four key areas to holistically serve and empower our community.

Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Prevention

We work to eliminate violence by increasing public accountability, strengthening social mechanisms, and implementing evidence-based prevention strategies to ensure safety and justice for all.

Mental Health Awareness

Dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for women, children, and vulnerable groups through mental health awareness campaigns and support strategies.

Safe Male Circumcision

We conduct programmes to support safe male circumcision, strengthening the rite of passage through medically sound and culturally respectful processes.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Focused on poverty relief by creating sustainable employment opportunities and encouraging self-employment through entrepreneurial skills development.
